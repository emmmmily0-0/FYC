About me

I'm Emily. I'm eager to become a digital artist who dedicates to culture preservation, protecting endangered natural environments, and discussing social issues with new media design.

I believe that instead of giving answers straight away, the works should guide the users to search for a meaning, leaving collective memories across generations, and stimulate social consciousness in order to advance the sustainable development and innovation of traditional culture.

I'm focused on emerging media today. I headed a graduation project on AR. My goal is to use the visual cues and immersive experiences enabled by new media (augmented reality, virtual reality, digital narratives, and multimedia), as well as the value of empathy to form a channel of social, cultural, media, and art communication. This notion drives me to become a digital artist.
